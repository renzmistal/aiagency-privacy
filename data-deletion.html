Data Deletion Instructions

Effective Date: May 9, 2025

If you have used the AIagency Facebook App and want to delete your data, you can request it by following these instructions:

How to Request Data Deletion

Send an email to our support team at aiagency.support@example.com with the subject line: “Facebook Data Deletion Request”.

Include the following information in your message:

  • Your full name
  • The email address linked to your Facebook account
  • Any relevant details to help us locate your data

We will process your request within 7 days and confirm once your data has been deleted from our servers.
